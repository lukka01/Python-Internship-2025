About us:

The main idea behind this project is to help users discover movies with great reviews and share their opinions in a respectful environment. Our platform allows you to explore movies, leave comments, and build your own list of favorites.

To keep the community friendly and safe, disrespectful comments are strictly forbidden. Users who violate the rules multiple times will be permanently blocked. This ensures a positive experience for everyone.

Only administrators are allowed to create, update, or delete movies, ensuring that all content remains accurate and reliable. Registered users can add movies to their favorites and share their thoughts through comments.

Enjoy discovering great movies and feel confident sharing your opinions with us.
